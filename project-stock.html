Project Overview

Designed an advanced predictive model for stock price movements using LSTM neural network, which significantly enhanced ability to forecast market trends. Using various time-series predictive model and conducted a comprehensive analysis on the relationship between pension funds and stock prices, sheding light on the impact of pension funds on stock prices. offering valuable insights for strategic decision-making. Refining the time intervals used in our LSTM model, achieved an 6% increase in accuracy, bolstering our forecasting capabilities.

Receiving assignment from the company, the purpose was to investigate the relationship between pension funds and stock prices. Through various modeling, trend analysis, and seasonality analysis, we concluded that there is not a significant correlation between them and that the pension fund is not an adequate strong feature to be used in price prediction. We initially thought that finding a specific correlation was the goal of this project, but the desired outcome form the company was to establish that there is no tremendous correlation between pensionf funds and stock prices. By producing the result they wanted, we were able to win an 2nd award.
